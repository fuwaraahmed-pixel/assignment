Frequently Asked Questions

Got questions? We've got answers.

How can I register for DevConf 2026?

You can easily register by clicking any of the "Register Now" or "Get Ticket" buttons on this landing page, which will redirect you to the registration page.

Are the workshops included in the Standard ticket?

The Standard ticket includes access to 2 workshop sessions. If you want unlimited access to all workshops, we recommend purchasing the PRO or Team ticket.

Can I get a refund if I cannot attend?

Yes, we offer full refunds up to 14 days before the event starts. Please contact our support team to process your request.

Will the sessions be recorded?

Yes! All technical talks and sessions will be recorded. Standard ticket holders get 30 days of digital access, while PRO and Team ticket holders get lifetime access.
